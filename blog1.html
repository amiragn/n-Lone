Suicide Prevention Hotline

Source: https://megapolitan.kompas.com/read/2019/11/18/15455261/cegah-bunuh-diri-dengan-nomor-darurat-berikut-ini?page=all

If you feel alone, believe me you are not. People with depression can complain and find a way out of the problems they face. Talking to community representatives can be an alternative if you don't want to see a psychiatrist or psychologist. If you have a tendency to commit suicide or you just want to complain about your problems and find a way out, you can call this emergency number. The following is a suicide prevention hotline that you can call:

- Hotline Kesehatan Jiwa Kemankes (021-500-454)
Number 500-454 is a number that was deliberately registered by the Ministry of Health to Telkom as a consulting service several years ago.

- Into The Light
Into The Light Indonesia is an inclusive community driven by young people across identities that upholds an evidence-based program approach and human rights. Into The Light focuses on suicide prevention and mental health for youth and other special populations. All activities carried out are in accordance with the motto Into The Light, namely to remove stigma, care for others, and love the soul. You can contact this community via email intothelight.email@gmail.com or go directly to the 'Assistance' page on their website https://intothelightid.wordpress.com/. In addition, you can search for them through their social media accounts on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram with @intolightid.

- LSM Jangan Bunuh Diri (021 9696 9293)
LSM Jangan Bunuh Diri is a non-governmental organization established as a form of concern for mental health. The purpose of the establishment of this community is to change the public's perspective on mental illness and correct myths and so that people understand that suicide is closely related to mental disorders or diseases. You can contact this community through the telephone number (021 0696 9293) or by e-mail jgandosadiri@yahoo.com.

- Get Happy
Get Happy Community is a community that focuses on depression, mental health, and stigma about mental illness in Indonesia. Get Happy strives to be a supportive community for people who experience depression, mental health disorders, or accompany those closest to them who experience this. Get Happy also seeks to neutralize public stigma on the issue of depression and mental health in Indonesia. You can contact this community via the site https://www.get-happy.org/ or you can also email get.happy.yuk@gmail.com

- Komunitas Peduli SKizofrenia Indonesia (KPSI)
The Indonesian Schizophrenia Care Community (KPSI) is a community that supports people with schizophrenia (ODS), families/caregivers, and people who care about mental health issues. These communities are spread throughout Indonesia and provide a deeper understanding of schizophrenia. This community opens vent and sharing sessions about mental health. For those of you who are interested in talking to this community, you can contact them via number 021-8514389 or visit the http://www.skizofrenia.org/ page and fill out the form there or through their Facebook and Twitter accounts at @KPSI_central.
