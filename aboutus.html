About Us

n-Lone is a technology-based free mental health service. You can do self-help by doing a validation test of your depression level and setting depression prevention goals as well as online counseling with storytellers if you want to share a problem you are facing, with a peer counselor if you want to get a solution from a peer's point of view, and even with a professional psychologist right from your smartphone. We believe that mental health services should be accessible to anyone, anytime, anywhere without mobility constraints.
